Update 5 - A Fresh Coat of Paint

This is the latest update to Satisfactory and the current stable version. Update 5 is exactly as the name suggests and it is a fresh coat of paint to the game with many enhancements and optimisations. Regarded by many in the aesthetic building community as the best update yet and allowing them to express their creativity more than ever before. This update also gave the multiplayer community some well-deserved attention with the release of dedicated servers.

Cosmetic changes include signs, brand new building materials such as cement, beams, windows and pillars. Painting and building factories have completely been revamped with the Customizer, patterns and build system changes which are now divided into soft and hard clearance. Vehicles have also seen significant optimisations as well as train collision and signalling systems being added to the game.

Update 4 - Drones

Update 4 brought one of the largest changes to the production, manufacturing, and transportation system of Satisfactory with the inclusion of Drones, a brand-new Tier 8 and improvements to UI, quality of life and networking. Nuclear and Aluminium production has been completely overhauled with the inclusion of the Blender manufacturing building. Drones can now deliver all the resources you need across the map in a very short time frame.

Lights have been added to the game and come in three different varieties to light up your factory in any color and brightness you desire. While you are at it, why not use the brand new Hover Pack to admire your factory from the sky or use the Zipline to traverse multiple factory levels in a way like never before.

Update 3 - Pipes

Just like the newest updates, Update 3 revamped the game in so many ways new and exciting ways. Before Update 3, fluids were transported in solid barrels on conveyor belts. This changed with the introduction of pipes and a revamp to fluids. A whole new level of complexity emerged as players had to manage pressure, head lift and a spaghetti of pipelines between the different production buildings â€“ and players loved the new challenge.

Along with pipes, new alternate recipes, The Awesome Shop, new building blocks, statues, hypertubes and a new starting area. The update featured many recipe rebalances as well as numerous small changes that just improved the game play experience tenfold.

Update 2 - Trains and Nuclear

Update 2 served to bring Satisfactory from its infancy into a game with much more sustenance and replay ability. Tier 7 was added to the game along with nuclear power which boasted a huge jump in complexity and power generation.

What if your nuclear power plant is on the opposite side of the map compared to your resource nodes? No problem just use the new trains to transport he vast quantities of resources needed extremely efficiently and quickly to its destination. New Biomes with increased exploration difficulty were added and hence the size of the map has greatly been increased.
