The Space Elevator

By far the largest and most impressive building in satisfactory, the Space Elevator serves one purpose and that is to send the extremely complex and resource intensive project parts into space to FICSIT INC. The player is required to make 3 project parts to unlock Tiers 3 and 4. These old project parts are then used in more complex project parts to unlock Tier 5 and 6. The process repeats for Tier 7 and 8 however these parts might require entire factories and hundreds of resources per minute.

Resource Extractors

Miner (Mk.1, Mk.2, Mk.3)

Miners are used to automatically extract solid resources from resource nodes such as iron and copper. Extracted resources are outputted directly to connected conveyor belts. Extraction speed is modified depending on node purity (impure, normal, pure). Mk.2 and Mk.3 miners are upgrades which are unlocked in later tiers and increase extraction speed.

Impure Node (p/min) Normal Node (p/min) Pure Node (p/min) Unlocks At
Miner Mk.1 30 60 120 Tier 0
Miner Mk.2 60 120 240 Tier 4
Miner Mk.3 120 240 480 Tier 8

Water Extractor

Extracts water from the body of water it is built on. Body of water needs to be deep enough and therefore rivers do not always suffice. Default extraction rate is 120m3/min and allows fluids to be pumped up to 10 meters upwards before auxiliary pumps are required.

Oil Extractor

Can be built on Oil Nodes to extract Crude Oil. Allows for crude oil to be pumped up to 10 meters upwards before auxiliary pumps are required. Extraction rates depend on node purity (Impure: 60m3/min ; Normal: 120m3/min ; Pure: 240m3/min)

Resource Well Pressurizer and Extractor

A Resource Well Pressurizer can be placed on the node of Water, Oil or Nitrogen Resource Wells. When activated, Resource Well Extractors can be placed on the pressurized satellite nodes to extract the resourcces. Extraction rates depend on the satellite node purity (Impure: 30m3/min ; Normal: 60m3/min ; Pure: 120m3/min)

Smelters

Smelter

The smelter has a single input and output which can be automatically fed with a conveyor belt. Ore is fed into the smelter to be produced into ingots. Rates vary depending on ore, recipe and overclocks applied. Unlocks at Tier 0.

Foundry

The foundry has two inputs and a single output which can be automatically fed with a conveyor belt. Two different resources or ore are smelted together to produce alloy ingots. Rates vary depending on ore, recipe and overclocks applied. Unlocks at Tier 3.

Manufacturers

Constructor

Constructors are the most basic manufacturing building, taking a single resource or part and crafting it into another part. Typically, large amounts of constructors are needed to make enough basic parts for later part production. Unlocks at Tier 0.

Assembler

The assembler is a step up from the constructor by making use of two inputs and a single output. Two basic parts can be combined in the assembler to create a moderately complex part, and this will be the workhorse of the mid-game. Unlocks at Tier 2.

Manufacturer

The manufacturer has four inputs and one output. Is used to craft three or four parts into a single complex part and it is used primarily in the late game to create all complex parts which do not require fluids. Unlocks at Tier 5.

Packager

The packager is used to package and unpackage fluids. It has both a conveyor belt and pipe input and output. The packager is used largely to package fluids into solid plastic containers for easier transport across large distances or elevations. Unlocks at Tier 5.

Refinery

The refinery is one of the most widely used buildings throughout the mid to ultra-late game. It has both a conveyor belt and pipe input and output. It is primarily used in the refining process of crude oil into other forms such as fuel, rubber and plastic, however it is also required in other recipes such as aluminium and getting extra resources from resource nodes. Unlocks at Tier 5.

Blender

The blender is used at the end of the most complex production lines. It has two conveyor belt inputs, two pipe inputs, one conveyor belt output and one pipe output. This building is used extensively in aluminium, battery, and nuclear fuel rod production. Unlocks at Tier 7 and requires adequate management of fluids to ensure that systems do not back up and stall.

Particle Accelerator

The Particle Accelerator is at the leading edge of production and is used in the recycling of spent nuclear fuel rods and manufacturing of plutonium fuel rods. Has two conveyor belt inputs, one fluid input and one conveyor belt output. It uses the most power of any building in Satisfactory – up to 1500MW which is as much as 20 blenders, the next most power intensive manufacturing building, therefore a stable power system is required. Unlocks at Tier 8 and is expected to manufacture many of the future parts still to be added to the game.

Power Generators

Biomass Burner

The biomass burner is the first power building and is unlocked at Tier 0. It burns any plant matter, such as leaves or wood which can be turned into biomass. Has no input and must therefore be handfed manually. Generates 30MW.

Coal Generator

The Coal generator is the first automated source of power and has one conveyor belt input and one pipe input. Uses coal to boil water and generates 75MW of power. Unlocks at Tier 3 and is the main source of power for the mid-game.

Fuel Generator

The Fuel Generator uses fuel or turbo-fuel to generate up to 150MW of Power. Has one pipe input for fuel. Unlocks at Tier 6 and is the main source of power in most factories until Nuclear Power is researched.

Geothermal Generator

The Geothermal Generator is built on a Geyser and is the only renewable source of power in the game. Power production fluctuates depending on the Geyser node placed on. Unlocks through research in the M.A.M.

Power Production

  • Impure 50-150 MW (100 MW average)
  • Normal 100-300 MW (200 MW average)
  • Pure 200-600 MW (400 MW average)

Power Storage

The Power Storage acts like a large-scale battery. It can be connected to a power grid to store excess power which can later be used in cases of high consumption. Can store 100MWh and is paired well with Geothermal Generators to balance out the fluctuating power output. Unlocks at Tier 4.

Nuclear Power Plant

The Nuclear Power Plant is unlocked at Tier 8 and is used to power mega-factories in the ultra-late game. Has one conveyor belt input and output as well as one pipe input. The Nuclear Power plant consumers Nuclear and Plutonium fuel rods to produce up to 2500MW. Requires 300m3/min of water for cooling and produces radioactive nuclear waste.
