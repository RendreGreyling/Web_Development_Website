About

Satisfactory is an early access game from Coffee Stain Studios who are based in Sweden. Satisfactory brings a unique first-person open-world factory building game set on an alien planet. You can play alone or with friends to create multi-level complex factories with a plethora of manufacturing buildings which are all connected with a maze of conveyors and pipes.

Lore

In Satisfactory you play as a Pioneer working for the conglomerate FICSIT Inc. who is dropped into an alien planet with only a handful of tools. Your goal is to harvest the planets natural resources to build low level factories which will become increasingly more complex as you progress and automate more processes. Once you have constructed the Space elevator, you can start manufacturing complex space elevator components which are sent up the space elevator for unknown purposes.

Early Access

As the game is still in early access, the lore is not yet fully implemented and has been hinted that it will come in the final release of Satisfactory. The developers have said that the game will remain in early access for some time to come as “Satisfactory is a big and complex game that can be expanded and refined for a very long time, and we believe that there is a community of factory builders out in the world that would love to join us to create this beast of a game together”.
