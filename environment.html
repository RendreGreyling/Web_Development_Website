Environment

Map

The map in Satisfactory consists of 19 different biomes. Of these you can start in 4 locations. These locations are: Grassfields, Rocky Desert, Northern Forest, Dune Desert. Between each biome you will often find enemies and some biomes like the swamp and Red Forest contain the most dangerous enemies and treacherous conditions which will offer a fierce challenge.

Resources are spread across all biomes, and you will typically find large amounts of low value resource nodes like Iron, Copper and Limestone in all biomes. Resource like Uranium and Bauxite have a high value and is required in many late game production lines, hence these resources are rare and can typically only be found in difficult to traverse areas.
